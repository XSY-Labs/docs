title: "x402 Gateways"
description: "HTTP 402 payment gateways for accepting UTY and yUTY over the x402 protocol."
tag: "Coming soon"
---

x402 gateway support is in development. It will let services price and accept UTY and yUTY payments over the [x402 protocol](https://x402.org), so HTTP clients — including AI agents — can settle requests against YieldPoint stablecoins natively.

Until it ships, the [Integration](/protocol/integration/overview) section documents the contract surface for direct on-chain payment flows.
