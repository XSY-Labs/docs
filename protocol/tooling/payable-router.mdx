title: "Payable Router"
description: "Router contract for accepting payments in arbitrary tokens and settling in UTY or yUTY."
tag: "Coming soon"
---

A payable router is in development. It will let merchants and applications accept payments in arbitrary tokens and settle in UTY or yUTY, with routing handled inside a single contract call.

Until it ships, the [Integration](/protocol/integration/overview) section covers direct deposit and redeem flows against the underlying vaults.
